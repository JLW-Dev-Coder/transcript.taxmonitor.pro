What This Workflow Solves

This is a repeatable workflow for tax professionals who want faster transcript reviews and cleaner client communication. It’s designed for consistency across staff, not “hero mode.”

Workflow Steps

  • Build the timeline (posted dates and key milestones).
  • Confirm the transcript type and the tax period.
  • Deliver next steps with a clear action/no-action split.
  • Flag holds, notices, and balance signals.
  • Translate codes into client language (one narrative, not a glossary).

Common Pitfalls

Common pitfalls

  • Letting the client steer the review with a single code they Googled.
  • Mixing multiple years and transcript types in one narrative.
  • Overpromising on IRS timelines or outcomes.
  • Skipping documentation (what you checked, what you concluded, why).
